Teaching Sand to Dream

Artificial Intelligence Explained

Dr. Jesse Wood

Teaching Sand to Dream is an introduction to artificial intelligence for readers with no mathematics or programming background. It traces the history and ideas of the field from the mid-century pioneers to modern large language models, treating the arguments and dead ends as the most interesting part of the story.
